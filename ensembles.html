The official hurricane track is represented by white, solid color lines for forecasts through 72 hours, while dashed lines represent forecasts through 72-120 hours. Track Variable Consensus Aids (TVCA) model consensus can be seen in red lines. Global Forecast System (GFS) are represented by cyan lines. Global Environmental Multiscale Model (GEMI) is represented by yellow lines.
